Memento Mori

04.01.2023
About the Project
I created a momento mori calendar using javascript, html, and css. The app shows the number of weeks I have left to live, and I used it as an opportunity to practice my skills in vanilla javascript by creating a small application. A momento mori is a reminder of death, and it is often used to encourage people to live their lives to the fullest and appreciate the time they have. My calendar is a unique take on this concept, and it serves as a reminder of my own mortality in a creative and interactive way.
